Our Story

Empowering India's Green Transition.

EcoTrack OS was born from a simple frustration: India's 63 million SMEs were being left behind in the global green economy because sustainability tools were built for corporations with dedicated ESG teams and massive budgets.

We built the first carbon intelligence platform made specifically for Bharat's real economy — from grocery stores in Lucknow to steel plants in Jharkhand.

  • State-specific grid emission factors from Central Electricity Authority
  • GHG Protocol Scope 1, 2 & 3 compliant calculations
  • EU CBAM & BEE audit-ready branded PDF reports
  • Fully offline — your data never leaves your device
  • Hindi + English bilingual interface for all team levels
